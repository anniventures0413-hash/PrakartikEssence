Usage guide

Simple natural routines.

Hair wash mix

Amla + Reetha + Shikakai + Bhringraj + Hibiscus. Mix with water, apply, rest, and rinse gently.

Face pack mix

Neem + Tulsi + Mulethi or Multani Mitti. Make a smooth paste, patch test, apply briefly, and rinse.

Floral care

FAQ

Common questions.

Are prices per 50gm?

Yes. Prices are per 50gm unless the product is liquid, like Gulab Jal, which is listed per 50ml.

Should customers patch test?

Yes. Natural ingredients can still irritate sensitive skin, so a patch test is recommended before use.
