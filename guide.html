Usage Guide

Simple routines for powders and botanicals.

These are customer-friendly usage ideas. Final wording can be adjusted once you decide exact product instructions.

Hair wash routine

Use Amla, Reetha, Shikakai, Bhringraj, and Hibiscus. Mix with water, apply to hair and scalp, rest briefly, and rinse thoroughly.

Face pack routine

Use Neem, Tulsi, Mulethi, Multani Mitti, Orange Peel, Cucumber, or Beetroot. Mix with rose water or plain water.

Floral routine

Use Dry Rose Leaves, Hibiscus, and Gulab Jal in refreshing care routines.

Safety

Usage reminders.

Patch test first

Apply a small amount on a small skin area before full use. Natural ingredients can still irritate sensitive skin.

Avoid eyes

Keep powders and mixtures away from eyes. Rinse immediately with clean water if contact happens.

Use fresh mixtures

Prepare small fresh batches instead of storing wet mixtures for long periods.
